2. Data Collected

We collect the following personal data in the indicated situations:

  • Contact form — name, email address, phone number (optional) and message.
  • ROI Calculator — values entered are for local calculation only and are not stored or transmitted.
  • Browsing data — IP address, browser type, pages visited and session duration, collected automatically via analytics cookies (see section 5).

3. Purpose and Legal Basis

Data is processed for the following purposes and legal bases:

  • Responding to contact requests — legal basis: performance of pre-contractual measures or contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Website traffic analysis and improvement — legal basis: user consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), obtained via the cookie banner.
  • Marketing communications — only with your explicit consent via opt-in. You may withdraw consent at any time.

4. Data Retention

Data submitted via the contact form is retained for the period necessary to respond to the request and, if a contractual relationship is established, for the legally required period (maximum 10 years for tax documentation). Analytics data is anonymised after 26 months.

5. Cookies

This website may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic website functionality. No consent required.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) — collect anonymised information about how visitors use the website. Only activated with your consent.
  • Advertising cookies (Google Ads / Meta Pixel) — used to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. Only activated with your consent.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via your browser settings or the opt-out tools provided by: Google Analytics Opt-out  |  Meta Cookies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes. Data may be shared with:

  • Technology service providers — such as email platforms (e.g. Google Workspace) acting as processors, bound by appropriate data protection agreements.
  • Advertising platforms — Google LLC and Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd., for conversion measurement and remarketing, only with your consent.
  • Competent authorities — when required by law or court order.

Where data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, we ensure adequate safeguards under GDPR (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions).

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — know what data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to object — object to processing for certain purposes, including direct marketing.
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to restriction — request suspension of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ola@payoffmarketing.pt. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority. In Portugal: CNPD. In the EU: your local data protection authority.

8. Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction or disclosure. The website uses secure connections (HTTPS). Internal access to data is restricted to staff who require it for their duties.

9. Links to Other Websites

This website may contain links to third-party websites. This privacy policy applies exclusively to our website. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any websites you visit.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time, in particular to reflect legislative changes or new data processing practices. The date of the last update is always shown below. For significant changes, we will notify users prominently on the website.
