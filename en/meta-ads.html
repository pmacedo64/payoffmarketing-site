The funnel we use

We don't just boost posts. We build funnels that warm up cold audiences and convert them systematically.

Top of Funnel — Awareness

Videos and creatives for cold audiences (interests, behaviours, lookalikes). Goal: visibility at low cost per impression.

↓

Mid Funnel — Consideration

Retargeting of people who engaged with ads or visited your site. Creatives with social proof and concrete benefits.

↓

Bottom of Funnel — Conversion

Direct ads for people already familiar with your brand. Clear offer, strong CTA, optimised landing page.

↓

Retention — Existing Customers

Upsell and cross-sell to your customer base via Customer Lists and ad sequences.
